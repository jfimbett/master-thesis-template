# Thesis Framing Skill

Use this skill when the user wants to choose, refine, or evaluate a master thesis topic in finance.

## Process

1. Identify the broad topic.
2. Propose 3 to 5 possible research questions.
3. For each question, assess:
   - feasibility,
   - data availability,
   - identification challenge,
   - finance relevance,
   - expected contribution.
4. Recommend the most feasible option.
5. Produce a one-page thesis concept note.

## Output format

Use this structure:

# Thesis Concept Note

## Working Title

## Research Question

## Motivation

## Contribution

## Data Needed

## Methodology

## Main Risks

## Next Steps

## Supervisor Pitch

Write a short paragraph the student could send to a supervisor.
